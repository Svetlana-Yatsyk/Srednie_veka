JOHANNES DE SACROBOSCO AND HIS DE SPHAERE:
NOTES TO TRANSLATION INTO RUSSIAN
This paper provides a translation of the first chapter of the Sphere by Johannes de Sacro-
bosco (early 137 с.). This work became one of the most well-known astronomical text-
books of the Middle Ages and early modern period. Hundreds of copies, both manuscripts
and printed ones, were produced during this time. The obscure biography of Sacrobosco
is observed in the preface. The intellectuals of the Middle Ages and early modern period
added a lot of details to it, the part of them is regarded as doubtful in modern science.

Besides, the sources that Sacrobosco used writing his Sphere are considered, so the cre-

ation of the Sphere is presented in the context of the evolution of medieval astronomy.

Keywords: Johannes de Sacrobosco, Sphere, medieval astronomy, history of science.
