The review discusses an edition of medieval podwody (i. e. duty of providing the
ruler and his officials with saddle horses) account records of the towns of Lesser
Poland, published by Marcin Starzynski and Michat Schmidt and comprising the re-
cords published earlier as well the hitherto unpublished ones. They are treated and
published mainly as the sources on the history of borough finances; other valuable
aspects of their information — on political, social, cultural history etc. — seem to be
underestimated by the publishers.

Keywords: podwody, account records, borough finances, Lesser Poland, Kingdom of
Poland, Jagiellonians.
