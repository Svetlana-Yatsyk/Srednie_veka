EARLY MODERNITY:
RETHINKING THE PERIOD’S BOUNDARY
The article is devoted to reflections on the boundaries of Early Modern period. In Rus-
sian historiography, there is a consensus associated with the definition of the chronologi-
cal framework of Early Modern Times in the late X V— late XVIII centuries. At the same
time, in recent years the foreign researchers have shown a tendency to shift the bound-
ary of Early Modern Times to the middle of the XIV century. The analysis of the politi-
cal processes that have taken place since this time in Europe leads to the necessity to the
rethinking the usual boundaries of early modern times.
Keywords: periodization, Middle Ages, Early Modernity, Western Europe.
