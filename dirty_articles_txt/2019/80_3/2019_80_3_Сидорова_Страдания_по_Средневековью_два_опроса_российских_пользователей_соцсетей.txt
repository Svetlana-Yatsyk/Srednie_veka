SUFFERING ON ACCOUNT OF THE MIDDLE AGES:
TWO SURVEYS OF RUSSIAN
SOCIAL NETWORKS USERS

The article presents the results of two surveys conducted among «VKontakte» and «Face-
book» users in May 2012 and in August 2019. The objective was to identify the sources of
information about the Middle Ages for ordinary people, to define the modern phenomena
that prompted people to seek for parallels with medieval history and to understand how
the image of the Middle Ages changed in collective consciousness during the last seven
years. 664 people took part in the survey in 2012 and 1627 people in 2019. The ratio of
females to males and the percentage of those aged 37—46 years have grown considerably.
